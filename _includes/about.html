About

Noah Sparkes is a UK-based composer specialising in music for film and TV. Working from his home studio, Noah has worked across a wide variety of genres from classical to jazz to hip-hop to experimental.

As well as producing for artists from an array of genres, Noah also writes sync music for agencies like MediaTracks.

Noah is also the recipient of the Gold Prize for Original Composition at the 2025 Saint-Saëns International Music Competition and was shortlisted for the Composer Spotlight Award at The Production Music Awards 2025.
